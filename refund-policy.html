Services and Refunds

Global Travelserve LLC offers professional travel consulting and SEO/digital marketing services. Due to the intellectual and labor-intensive nature of our services, our refund policy is governed by the executed Service Agreement or Statement of Work (SOW) signed by the client.

Policy for Digital Services (SEO/Consulting)

  • **Pre-Service Commencement:** Full refund is available if requested in writing within 7 days of payment and before any work has commenced on the project.
  • **Post-Service Commencement:** Once work has begun (including strategy development, research, or audit initiation), fees are non-refundable. Any termination of service will be subject to the terms outlined in the binding Service Agreement.

Policy for Travel Consulting Fees

  • Consulting fees are typically non-refundable once the planning phase has begun, as they compensate for the expert time and advice provided.
  • In certain cases, a credit for future services may be offered at the sole discretion of Global Travelserve LLC.
