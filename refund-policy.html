Refund Policy

Last updated: January 02, 2026

Thank you for choosing Global Travelserve LLC. We strive to provide the best services possible. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Refunds

Our refund policy lasts 30 days for consulting services. If 30 days have passed since your purchase, we can’t offer you a full refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your service must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.

Refunds for Services

Once we receive your request for a refund, we will inspect it and notify you on the status of your refund.

If the refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or original method of payment). You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Shipping

Since we provide digital and consulting services, there are no shipping costs involved.

Contact Us

If you have any questions on how to request a refund, contact us at support@globaltravelserve.world.
