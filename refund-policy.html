Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Consulting Services

At Global Travelserve LLC, we provide professional SEO and Travel Logistics consulting. Because our services are based on labor, expertise, and time-intensive analysis, consulting fees are generally non-refundable once the work (audit, strategy session, or research) has commenced.

2. Travel Booking & Deposits

Deposits for travel itineraries are subject to the terms of the specific airline, hotel, or third-party provider. Global Travelserve LLC acts as a consultant; therefore, any refunds for actual travel bookings are governed by the underlying service provider's policy.

3. Cancellation Notice

  • Cancellations made 72 hours prior to a scheduled consulting session: 100% credit toward future services.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours prior: Subject to a 50% "Late Cancellation" fee.

4. Dispute Resolution

We aim for 100% satisfaction. If you are unhappy with a consulting deliverable, please contact support@globaltravelserve.world within 7 days. We will work to rectify the strategy at no additional cost.
